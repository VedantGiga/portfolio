AutoBrief AI is a full-stack, AI-powered SaaS application designed to automate daily work summaries for individuals and remote teams. The platform connects with tools like GitHub, Slack, Jira, Notion, and Google Calendar to fetch a user's daily activity and generates concise, human-readable summaries using LLM-based AI (OpenAI/Gemini/Claude). These summaries are delivered via email and also available on a sleek, modern dashboard. The project is built with scalability, automation, and clean UI/UX in mind, aiming to reduce unnecessary meetings and improve team visibility in asynchronous work environments.
